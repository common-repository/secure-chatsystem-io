== Description ==

ChatSystem.io is the real time LeadChat.com plugin for loading the Lead Chat service. Having a full-time team of live chat agents focussed on lead conversion from your traffic, along with analytics technology to optimise sentiment, language and proactive engagement, is all integrated onto your Wordpress site via this plugin.

The plugin allows the Chat System widget to be placed across all pages of your website

LeadChat Features
	Over 100,000 Leads Generated each month

	24 hours a day, seven days a week coverage

	Complete Lead conversion engine.

	We customise the look and feel of the widget branding
	
	API to push the lead data directly into your CRM

	Full lead management portal with access to all leads, transitions and report data

	We are experts at learning your business to ensure we deliver an exceptional outcome, everytime.

	Continuous chat experience optimization led by our in-house experts in neurolinguistics, data science, and behavioural psychology.

	Our chat agents uncover insights into customer sentiment, product opportunities, website traffic quality feedback and more.

	Our dedicated QA team hand read every single one of the hundreds of thousands of chat transcripts for all clients, every day.


== Installation ==

1. Upload `chatsystmio` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to ChatSystem Setting menu under Setting menu in WordPress
